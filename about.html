About

Hello. I am Malladi Subrahmanyam, a government servant with more than 30 years of service.

In my spare time, I read Telugu Stories and listen to music, particularly those of Ghantasala, Ramakrishna, P Susheela, S. Janaki and the like in the old Telugu Films. I am particularly fond of Ghantasala's rendition of the Bhagavadgeeta. I am also interested in Hinduism and the philosophy of Adi Shankaracharya.

So, some of what I will be posting here will be related to these topics.
